# Privacy Policy for Candles

**Last Updated: April 19, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how the **Candles** mobile application (candle flame ambience and settings), developed by **Diego de Souza Castro**, collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application, available on the App Store and Google Play.

## 1. Your Candle Settings Stay on Your Device

The app stores your **preferences locally on your device**, including for example:

- Flame colour (hue), intensity, flicker speed, and visual style  
- Screen brightness preference (when you adjust it in the app)  
- Options such as whether the flame follows device tilt or the screen stays awake  

This information is stored using **on-device storage** (for example SharedPreferences). **We do not operate a backend that receives or stores your flame settings on our servers.** We have no access to this data unless you contact us for support and choose to share information voluntarily.

## 2. Information We Collect

### Device Sensor (Motion)

The app may use the **device accelerometer** (motion sensor) so the candle flame can **tilt with gravity** when that feature is enabled. Motion readings are processed **on your device** to compute orientation; they are **not uploaded** to our servers as part of the app’s normal operation.

### Usage and Analytics Data (Firebase Analytics)

We use **Google Analytics for Firebase** to understand **aggregated** app usage. Depending on how you use the app, this may include:

- **App opens** and **screen views** (for example which screens you navigate to)  
- **Flame style usage**: we may log which **flame visual style** is active (for example geometric, particles, procedural) and whether that reflects **settings loaded at launch** or a **choice you made in the settings sheet** — **not** free-text content  
- Other **technical or engagement events** we configure to improve the product, designed **not** to include personal messages or sensitive user-typed content (this app does not include a chat or similar text feature)

These analytics events are intended to help us understand **which features are used** and how the app performs in aggregate.

### Diagnostic Data (Firebase Crashlytics)

We use **Firebase Crashlytics** to improve stability. It may collect **crash reports and handled errors**, including:

- Stack traces and technical error messages when the app fails or when certain errors are reported  
- Device and app version information useful to diagnose issues  

Crashlytics reports are **not** designed to include your name, email, or the specific numeric values of every slider unless such data were accidentally included in an error message (we aim to avoid that).

### Advertising (Google AdMob)

The app uses **Google AdMob** to display advertisements (for example **interstitial** ads and **app open** ads). AdMob may collect data such as **device identifiers**, **approximate location** (at a regional level where applicable), and **ad interaction** data in accordance with [Google’s Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy). AdMob does **not** receive your flame settings as a structured “profile” from us beyond what the advertising SDKs collect under Google’s policies.

### Other Usage Data

We may collect other **non-personal or aggregated** information related to how the app is used, such as:

- Device type and operating system  
- App version  
- General usage patterns  

This data helps us improve performance and user experience.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

The collected data is used solely for:

- Improving the app’s experience, stability, and features  
- Monitoring performance and diagnosing crashes  
- Understanding aggregated usage (for example which flame styles are popular)  

We do **not** sell your personal information. We do not use your data to train third-party AI models (this app does not provide cloud AI chat).

## 4. Third-Party Services

### Google AdMob

Advertising is provided by **Google AdMob**. Data collection and use are governed by [Google’s Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) and the choices Google offers for ads personalization where available on your platform.

### Google Firebase (Analytics and Crashlytics)

- **Analytics**: as described in Section 2, under [Google’s Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) and [Firebase / Google Analytics terms](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy).  
- **Crashlytics**: as described in Section 2, under the same Firebase / Google policies.

### Other Services

Any additional third-party tools we add will be listed in updates to this policy and will operate under their respective privacy policies.

## 5. Data Security

We rely on **industry-standard practices** from Apple, Google, and Firebase for data handled by those platforms. **Settings you keep only on the device** remain subject to your device’s own security (passcode, encryption, backups).

No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. We recommend keeping your device updated and protected with a secure lock.

## 6. Children’s Privacy

The app is **not directed at children under 13**. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps.
